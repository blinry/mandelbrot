What is the Mandelbrot Set?

To explain, consider the following situation: You have a bunny, and a flag. You can place the flag everywhere you want, while the bunny has its own free will. It's still asleep... z z Z Z Z.

Drag the flag around. No surprises here.

Ah, the bunny woke up! The first thing it will always do is walk to the position of the flag. We call this the *first step*. All steps after that consist of two movements: First, it will *hop*! The bunny has a very particular way of hopping: It looks at the angle it has to the right-direction and hops in such a way that its angle doubles. It also looks at the distance to its sleeping position and hops so that its distance to it *squares*. Intuitively, if its distance to its sleeping position is less than 1, it will hop a little bit closer, and if the distance islarger than 1, it will hop a little bit farther away.

Pull the step slider up. Step 1 is the initial walking. Step 2 is the hop! Then drag the flag around.

And after that, it will walk again, in the same direction and distance it initially walked towards the flag:

Pull the slider up. Step 3 is another walking.
